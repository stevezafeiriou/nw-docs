## Overview

This website is the public documentation for the [Nostalgie World](https://nostalgie.world) ecosystem. It combines three user-facing guides in one place:

- **Wall of Nostalgia** for Wall users
- **Operations Dashboard** for client users who manage mirrors
- **Mirror Installations** for clients and operators who use physical Nostalgie mirrors

It is written as practical help content focusing on what users can do, what they will see, and how to complete common tasks.

Use this website to understand:

- how to access each part of the Nostalgie World experience
- how to complete the main user flows
- how credits, profiles, badges, mirrors, and settings work at a user level
- how to troubleshoot common problems
- which workflows are included publicly, and which internal admin or engineering workflows are not covered

This documentation is intended for **Wall users**, **clients**, and **mirror operators**.

## Start here

Wall of Nostalgia is a public Nostalgie World ecosystem app. It is where you create your profile, build your avatar, post **Beacons**, collect badges, and explore the community. This guide is written as user help, focused on what you can do in the app.

If you are new, follow this order:

1. Sign in
2. Complete onboarding
3. Create your avatar
4. Open **Posts**, **Profile**, **Settings**, and **Leaderboard**

> **Important**
> You should finish onboarding and save an avatar before you start using the app.

## What you can do in Wall of Nostalgia

You can use Wall of Nostalgia to:

- create your public profile
- build and customize your avatar
- post public Beacons\*
- browse community content
- collect badges
- redeem gift codes
- manage credits and settings

\*A Beacon is a short public message of love, affirmation, support, or encouragement that you share with the Wall of Nostalgia community. It is meant for kind, uplifting, and emotionally warm content.

## Create your account

You can sign in with:

- Google
- email magic link

After you sign in, the app checks whether you still need to:

- complete onboarding
- create an avatar

## Complete onboarding

Onboarding is the first-time setup flow. It includes:

- a welcome step
- setting your display name
- moving into avatar setup
- finishing your account setup

If you leave before finishing, return and continue until setup is complete.

## Create your avatar

Your avatar is part of your public identity in the app. You need one before you can fully use Wall of Nostalgia.

### To create your avatar

1. Open the avatar setup flow.
2. Choose your avatar style.
3. Customize the available parts.
4. Preview your avatar.
5. Save it.

Some avatar items are free. Premium items use paid credits.

### Download your avatar

You can download your avatar from the **Profile** page.

1. Open **Profile**.
2. Find the avatar preview.
3. Click the **eye icon**.
4. Download the image.

## Post a Beacon

A **Beacon** is a public post you share in Wall of Nostalgia.

### Before you post

- Your message can be up to **200 characters**
- You may be asked to choose a feeling before publishing
- Some posts may include more of your profile context than others

### To post a Beacon

1. Open **Posts**.
2. Write your message.
3. Keep it within **200 characters**.
4. Choose your feeling.
5. Review your post.
6. Publish it.

## Understand credits

Wall of Nostalgia uses two kinds of credits.

### Free allowance

Your **free allowance** is the number of free credits you receive each month for posting.

- it refreshes on the **1st of each month**
- it is meant for your regular posting activity

### Paid credits

**Paid credits** are used when:

- you have already spent your monthly free allowance
- you unlock premium avatar items

## Manage your profile

Your profile can include:

- display name
- avatar
- bio
- social links
- badges

### To update your profile

1. Open **Profile**.
2. Edit the details you want to change.
3. Save your changes.

## Use gift codes

If you receive a gift code, you can redeem it in the app.

### To redeem a gift code

1. Open **Settings**.
2. Find the gift code section.
3. Enter the code.
4. Confirm the result shown on screen.

## Claim badges

You may be able to claim badges through event links or badge scan flows.

### To claim a badge

1. Open the badge claim or scan flow.
2. Scan the code or open the badge link.
3. Follow the prompts.
4. Review the result.

## View notifications

End users can receive notifications related to:

- badges
- credits
- activity

Open the notifications area in the app to review them.

## Use the leaderboard

The **Leaderboard** lets you view top community activity and compare progress.

### To open the leaderboard

1. Open **Leaderboard**.
2. Review the rankings.
3. Return to **Profile** to track your own badges and progress.

## Common problems

### Why can’t I open the full app yet?

You probably still need to complete onboarding or save your avatar first.

### Why do some posts show older profile details?

Some posts keep the profile details that existed when they were published. This is expected behavior.

### Why can’t I post?

Check whether:

- you finished onboarding
- you created your avatar
- you still have free allowance or paid credits available

### Why didn’t my gift code work?

The code may be invalid, expired, inactive, or already used. Enter it again carefully and review the message shown in the app.

## FAQ

### When do free credits refresh?

Your free posting allowance refreshes on the **1st of each month**.

### What are paid credits used for?

Paid credits are used after your monthly free allowance is spent, and for premium avatar items.

### Can I filter posts by time range?

No. Time-based filters are not part of the Wall of Nostalgia user experience.

### Can I download my avatar?

Yes. Open **Profile** and click the **eye icon** on the avatar preview.

# Operations Dashboard

Operations Dashboard is the client control center for Nostalgie mirrors. It helps you manage your mirrors, update mirror settings, review account status, and control what appears on each mirror. This public guide focuses on the client experience, not internal admin workflows.

## Start here

Use Operations Dashboard if you need to:

- access your client workspace
- manage one or more mirrors
- pair a mirror
- update mirror settings
- hide or restore content on a mirror
- review billing status

> **Note**
> An admin can override client settings when needed.

## Sign in and access your workspace

After sign-in, the dashboard sends you to the right area based on your access level. Depending on your account, you may see:

- the client dashboard
- a client access request flow
- general account settings

## Request client access

If your account does not yet have client access:

1. Open the client request page.
2. Enter your business details.
3. Submit your request.
4. Wait for approval.
5. Return after access has been granted.

## Manage your mirrors

Each mirror belongs to your client account. From the client dashboard, you can:

- open your mirror list
- select a mirror
- update its settings
- pair it
- activate or deactivate it
- moderate what appears on that mirror

## Open your mirror list

1. Sign in.
2. Open the client dashboard.
3. Go to **Mirrors**.
4. Select the mirror you want to manage.

## Pair a mirror

If a mirror is not yet connected, use the pairing flow in the dashboard.

### To pair a mirror

1. Open the mirror pairing or connect flow.
2. Scan the mirror using the pairing tool.
3. Follow the steps shown in the dashboard.
4. Wait for the mirror to connect.

## Update mirror settings

You can manage all available settings for the **legacy theme** in the client dashboard.

### To update mirror settings

1. Open **Mirrors**.
2. Select the mirror.
3. Open **Settings**.
4. Review the available controls.
5. Save your changes.

> **Warning**
> The refresh rate can be edited, but you should only change it if you understand what it does. If you are unsure, leave it as it is.

## Hide or restore content on one mirror

Mirror moderation only affects the mirror you are currently managing. It does not change content everywhere else.

### To hide or restore content

1. Open the mirror.
2. Go to its moderation area.
3. Find the content you want to change.
4. Hide it or restore it.

## Activate or deactivate a mirror

You can activate or deactivate a mirror from the mirror settings area.

### To change mirror status

1. Open the mirror in the dashboard.
2. Go to **Settings**.
3. Choose **Activate** or **Deactivate**.
4. Save your changes.

## Check billing status

For now, Operations Dashboard uses two billing states:

- **Active**
- **Inactive**

### To review billing

1. Open **Billing**.
2. Check whether the account is **Active** or **Inactive**.
3. Follow the on-screen instructions if action is needed.

## Notifications

Client users can receive more operational notifications than regular Wall users. These may include account, mirror, or system-related updates.

## Common problems

### Why can’t I access the client dashboard?

You may not have client access yet. Submit a client access request and wait for approval.

### Why can’t I save mirror settings?

Check whether:

- your subscription is active
- the values you entered are valid
- you changed something advanced by mistake

### Why should I be careful with refresh rate?

Refresh rate changes can affect mirror behavior. If you do not know what a different value will do, do not change it.

### Why doesn’t hiding content remove it everywhere?

Mirror moderation only affects the specific mirror you are editing.

## FAQ

### Who should use Operations Dashboard?

Clients who manage Nostalgie mirrors.

### Are admin workflows included in this public documentation?

No. Admin-only workflows are not published here.

### Can an admin override client settings?

Yes.

### What billing states are shown right now?

For now, the dashboard uses **Active** and **Inactive**.

### Can I change mirror settings myself?

Yes. You can manage the available **legacy theme** settings from the client dashboard.

# Mirror Installations

Mirror Installations covers the day-to-day use of a physical Nostalgie mirror. This guide explains how to connect a mirror, manage it through the dashboard, and understand its normal states in plain language. It is written for clients and operators, not engineers.

## Start here

Use this section if you need to:

- set up a new mirror
- pair a mirror
- activate or deactivate a mirror
- update mirror settings
- understand what to expect from a connected mirror

## What a mirror does

A Nostalgie mirror is a full-screen display that shows content based on the settings and controls chosen in Operations Dashboard. Once connected, it follows the mirror configuration saved for your account.

## Before you begin

Make sure:

- the mirror is powered on
- you can access Operations Dashboard
- the mirror belongs to the correct client account

## Pairing

### What you will see before connection

If the mirror is not connected yet, it shows:

- **pairing**

### Pair a new mirror

1. Turn on the mirror.
2. Wait until the screen shows **pairing**.
3. Open Operations Dashboard.
4. Start the mirror pairing flow.
5. Follow the pairing steps in the dashboard.
6. Wait for the mirror to finish connecting.

## Update mirror settings

All available settings for the **legacy theme** can be managed from Operations Dashboard.

### To change mirror settings

1. Open the mirror in Operations Dashboard.
2. Go to **Settings**.
3. Review the available options.
4. Change only the values you understand.
5. Save your changes.

> **Warning**
> Some settings can strongly affect how the mirror behaves. If you are unsure what a setting does, do not change it.

## Activate or deactivate a mirror

Clients can activate or deactivate a mirror from the settings dashboard.

### To deactivate a mirror

1. Open the mirror in Operations Dashboard.
2. Go to **Settings**.
3. Choose **Deactivate**.
4. Save your changes.

### To reactivate a mirror

1. Open the mirror in Operations Dashboard.
2. Go to **Settings**.
3. Choose **Activate**.
4. Save your changes.

## What to expect from a connected mirror

A connected mirror should:

- display content automatically
- follow the settings saved in the dashboard
- reflect the current mirror configuration

If something looks wrong, first check:

- whether the mirror is active
- whether it was paired successfully
- whether the latest settings were saved in the dashboard

## Common problems

### Why is the mirror still showing “pairing”?

The mirror has not finished connecting yet. Open Operations Dashboard and complete the pairing steps again if needed.

### Why isn’t the mirror behaving the way I expect?

Check whether:

- the mirror is active
- the correct settings were saved
- a recent change affected mirror behavior

### Why shouldn’t I change settings I don’t understand?

Some settings can noticeably change the mirror experience. If you are unsure, leave them unchanged.

## FAQ

### What does the mirror show before it is connected?

It shows **pairing**.

### Can clients manage mirrors themselves?

Yes. Clients can manage mirrors from Operations Dashboard.

### Can clients activate or deactivate mirrors?

Yes. This is done from the mirror settings area in Operations Dashboard.

### Should debug tools be documented publicly?

No. Debug tools are not part of the public documentation.

### Which mirror settings are included in public docs?

All available settings for the **legacy theme** are included for client users.
